Octoquill: The Future of Creative Writing

Timeline

Deliverables

Web UI, Branding, Design System

Tags

Writing, Community, SaaS

Project Overview

Octoquill is a platform designed for writers to publish their work in a distraction-free environment while building a community. The goal was to create a unique, inspiring UI that reflects the creative nature of the users and fosters meaningful engagement.

Problem Statement

Most writing platforms are either too cluttered with ads and social features or too clinical and uninspiring. Writers needed a space that felt "creative" and "organic" without sacrificing modern usability and performance.

My Role & Responsibilities

Product Design

Defining the end-to-end user experience for writers and readers.

Branding

Developing the "Octoquill" visual identity, including logo and color palette.

Design System

Building a library of reusable components for a consistent experience.

Design Process

01

Discovery

Exploring the psychological needs of writers and creative thinkers.

02

Ideation

Brainstorming "Focus Mode" features and community interaction models.

03

Design

Creating high-fidelity mockups with a focus on typography and whitespace.

04

Refinement

Polishing micro-interactions and ensuring accessibility across devices.

Key Design Decisions

Typography-First UI

Prioritized readability by selecting high-quality serif fonts and optimizing line heights for long-form reading.

Immersive Focus Mode

Designed a writing interface that fades away all UI elements when the user starts typing, leaving only the words.
