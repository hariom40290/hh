Project Overview

Patel Lab & Clinic is a comprehensive healthcare management system designed to simplify patient registration, appointment scheduling, and report management. The goal was to reduce manual errors and improve the overall patient experience.

Problem Statement

The clinic was using a manual paper-based system that was slow, prone to data loss, and caused long waiting times for patients. Managing records and tracking appointments was becoming a bottleneck for the staff.

My Role & Responsibilities

User Flow Design

Mapping out the patient journey from registration to report collection.

Dashboard UI

Designing a clean, efficient dashboard for clinic administrators and doctors.

Prototyping

Creating high-fidelity prototypes to demonstrate the system's functionality.

Design Process

01

Research

Interviewing clinic staff to understand their daily challenges.

02

Wireframing

Developing low-fidelity layouts for the patient registration portal.

03

Visual Design

Applying a clean, medical-themed aesthetic with a focus on clarity.

04

Evaluation

Reviewing designs with medical professionals for usability and accuracy.

Key Design Decisions

Simplified Forms

Reduced the number of fields in the registration form to the absolute essentials to speed up the process.

Color-Coded Status

Used a clear color-coding system for appointment statuses (e.g., Pending, Completed, Cancelled) for quick scanning.

Final UI Showcase

Impact & Outcome

60%

Reduction in patient waiting time during peak hours.

100%

Digital record-keeping achieved, eliminating paper waste and data loss.
