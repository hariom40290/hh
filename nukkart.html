Nukkart: Local Commerce Reimagined

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

2 Months

Deliverables

Mobile App UI, Branding

Tags

E-commerce, Mobile, Local

Project Overview

Nukkart is a mobile application designed to bring local neighborhood stores online. It empowers small business owners with digital tools to reach their customers directly, providing a seamless shopping experience for the community.

Problem Statement

Small local retailers were losing customers to large e-commerce giants because they lacked an online presence. Customers wanted the convenience of online shopping but preferred supporting their local "Nukkad" stores.

My Role & Responsibilities

User Research

Interviewing local shop owners and neighborhood shoppers.

Visual Identity

Creating a friendly, approachable brand identity for the app.

Mobile UI

Designing an intuitive mobile interface for both buyers and sellers.

Design Process

01

Empathize

Understanding the pain points of non-tech-savvy shop owners.

02

Define

Mapping out the core features: inventory, orders, and payments.

03

Ideate

Sketching simple navigation patterns for quick actions.

04

Prototype

Building a clickable prototype for field testing.

Key Design Decisions

Icon-First Navigation

Used large, recognizable icons to help users who might struggle with small text or technical jargon.

Localized Language Support

Designed the UI to support multiple local languages to ensure inclusivity for all retailers.
