Tags

Project Overview

Fusion24X7 is an enterprise-level CRM designed to streamline business workflows and enhance team collaboration. The platform focuses on data visualization, task automation, and real-time analytics for high-performance teams.

Problem Statement

Existing CRM solutions were either too complex for small teams or too limited for large enterprises. Users struggled with data overload and fragmented workflows, leading to decreased productivity and missed opportunities.

My Role & Responsibilities

UX Strategy

Defining user personas and mapping out complex workflow journeys.

UI Design

Creating a scalable design system and high-fidelity dashboard interfaces.

Prototyping

Building interactive prototypes for stakeholder feedback and user testing.

Design Process

01

Research

Analyzing competitor tools and interviewing power users.

02

Wireframing

Iterating on low-fidelity layouts to define information hierarchy.

03

Visual Design

Applying the brand identity and creating a modern, clean aesthetic.

04

Testing

Validating designs with real users and refining based on feedback.

Key Design Decisions

Modular Dashboard

Implemented a widget-based system allowing users to customize their view based on their specific role.

Dark Mode Optimization

Carefully selected a navy-blue palette to reduce eye strain for users spending long hours on the platform.
